How to Use Engine Pawn

  1. Move pieces on the board to create any position.
  2. This can be from a real game, puzzle, or chaos.
  3. Click Analyze Position.
  4. The analyst on the right gives commentary.
  5. Toggle board mode if needed.

Engine Pawn is not trying to be Stockfish. It’s trying to be the friend who loudly evaluates your blunders.
